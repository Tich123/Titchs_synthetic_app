Scalping Strategy

72% Win Rate

Fast-paced trading strategy using 8-period EMA and Stochastic oscillator for quick entries and exits. Ideal for capturing small price movements in volatile markets.

Timeframe
5 minutes
Risk:Reward
1:1.5
Indicators
8 EMA, Stochastic
Best Markets
Vol 25, Vol 50

Entry Rules

• Price above 8 EMA indicating short-term uptrend
• Stochastic drops below 20 then crosses back up
• Enter on next candle after Stochastic crossover

Swing Trading

68% Win Rate

Medium-term strategy using moving average crossovers and RSI to capture larger price movements. Perfect for traders who prefer fewer trades with higher profit potential.

Timeframe
1-4 hours
Risk:Reward
1:2
Indicators
20 SMA, 50 SMA, RSI
Best Markets
Vol 10, Vol 25

Entry Rules

• 20 SMA crosses above 50 SMA (bullish crossover)
• RSI above 50 but below 70 (momentum without overbought)
• Wait for pullback to 20 SMA for entry

Sniper Entry

65% Win Rate

Precision-based strategy using fair value gaps and market structure analysis for high-probability entries. Designed for patient traders seeking optimal risk-reward ratios.

Timeframe
15m - 1 hour
Risk:Reward
1:3
Indicators
FVG, BOS, Liquidity
Best Markets
Vol 50, Vol 75

Entry Rules

• Identify fair value gap (FVG) on chart
• Wait for price to return to FVG zone
• Look for break of structure confirmation

Mean Reversion

70% Win Rate

Counter-trend strategy using Bollinger Bands and oscillators to identify overextended price movements. Excellent for range-bound markets and volatility mean reversion.

Timeframe
30m - 4 hours
Risk:Reward
1:1.8
Indicators
BB, MACD, RSI
Best Markets
Vol 10, Vol 25

Entry Rules

• Price touches outer Bollinger Band
• MACD shows divergence from price
• RSI in overbought/oversold territory
